Know Your Own Strengths

By Michael Fawzy, a Front-End Ninja

Studies have proved that human mental abilities are unlimited but we only use a very limited part of it. This means that we, humans, could do anything we wish for, even if our physical abilities don’t match. We can simply invent a tool that help us do the job.

So, however impossible your dream seems, all you need is to do is find out the skills needed to achieve it. If you don’t have them, you can simply acquire them.

Know where you are, find out where you want to go and how to go there, then add a little faith and perseverance. In no time you will find yourself there.

Remember! Your abilities are unlimited, so use them..
